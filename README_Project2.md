# 📊 Sales Analytics Dashboard — Excel + Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
End-to-end sales analytics project analyzing **10,000+ sales records** across products, regions, and customer segments. Built an interactive dashboard tracking revenue, profit, and KPIs using Excel and Power BI.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered
| # | Question |
|---|---|
| 1 | What is the total revenue, profit, and average order value? |
| 2 | Which product category drives the most revenue? |
| 3 | How is revenue trending month over month? |
| 4 | Which region contributes the most sales? |
| 5 | Who are the top 10 highest-spending customers? |
| 6 | What is the profit margin across categories? |

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used
| Tool | Usage |
|---|---|
| **Excel** | Data cleaning, SUMIF, COUNTIF, pivot-style summary tables, charts |
| **Power BI** | DAX measures, interactive dashboards, slicers, KPI cards |
| **Functions** | SUMIF, COUNTIF, AVERAGE, INDEX-MATCH, COUNTA |
| **DAX** | SUM, DIVIDE, AVERAGE measures |

---

## 📊 Key Findings
- **Electronics** is the top revenue category — contributes ~72% of total sales
- **Laptops** are the highest revenue product consistently across months
- **North region (Delhi)** leads in revenue contribution
- Revenue grew month-over-month from Jan → Apr 2024
- **Credit Card** is the most preferred payment method (40%+ orders)
- Profit margin averages **~18-20%** across all categories

---

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Open `Sales_Analytics_Dashboard.xlsx` in Microsoft Excel
2. Navigate to **Summary Analysis** sheet for KPIs and charts
3. For Power BI dashboard — follow steps in **Power BI Steps** sheet
4. Import **Raw Data** sheet into Power BI Desktop
